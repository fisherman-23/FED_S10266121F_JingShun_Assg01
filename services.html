About Us

At IKEA, we're more than just a furniture company—we're a global home furnishing brand with a unique vision of creating a better everyday life for the many people. Founded in 1943 by Ingvar Kamprad in Sweden, our philosophy has always been about offering well-designed, functional home furnishing products at prices so low that as many people as possible can afford them. We believe that good design should be accessible to everyone, not just a privileged few. Our democratic design approach combines form, function, quality, sustainability, and low price, ensuring that every product tells a story of innovation and thoughtful design. With over 460 stores in more than 60 countries, we've grown from a small Swedish mail-order business to a global home furnishing brand that serves millions of customers worldwide. Our team of designers, engineers, and creative thinkers work together to develop smart, practical solutions that make everyday life easier and more enjoyable. At IKEA, we're not just selling furniture; we're helping people transform their living spaces and create homes that reflect their personality, dreams, and aspirations.

Our Sustainability Pledge

At IKEA, sustainability isn't just a buzzword—it's a fundamental commitment that drives everything we do. We recognize our responsibility to the planet and future generations, which is why we've set ambitious goals to become fully circular and climate positive by 2030. This means dramatically reducing our carbon footprint, using only renewable and recycled materials, and designing products that can be reused, repaired, and ultimately recycled.

Our approach goes beyond just manufacturing. We're transforming our entire business model to promote sustainable living for our customers. From solar panels on our store roofs to offering affordable, energy-efficient home solutions, we're helping people reduce their environmental impact without compromising on design or functionality. We believe that by making sustainable choices accessible and attractive, we can inspire millions of people to join us in creating a more sustainable future. Our vision is not just to sell furniture, but to enable people to live better, more sustainable lives within the limits of our planet.
